My Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio page. Here, you will find some of my best projects, showcasing my skills and creativity in web development, UI/UX design, and programming. My work ranges from simple static web pages to complex web applications using the latest technologies.

Project 1: Natural Water Purifier

Led a team in the development of a model utilizing solar energy and an open field to purify water. Presented the project at the National Science and Technology Fair 2018, where it received a nomination. Demonstrated a strong understanding of renewable energy systems and their application in sustainable water purification.

Project 2: Invisible Cloth Project

Designed and developed a Python project called "Invisible Cloth" for experimental purposes. Created a code that effectively removes the red color from the screen, allowing for the customization of the target color. Demonstrated strong proficiency in Python programming, problem-solving, and creative thinking.
