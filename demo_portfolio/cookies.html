Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, improve site functionality, and analyze traffic patterns. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize returning users and remember preferences, ensuring a smoother browsing experience.

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, you may disable cookies in your browser settings, but please note that some features may not function properly without them. We use different types of cookies, including session cookies that expire when you close your browser and persistent cookies that remain on your device for a set period.

We utilize third-party cookies to collect insights into user behavior, enabling us to refine content and services. These cookies help us understand which pages are most visited and how users interact with our site. We do not use cookies to collect personal data that can identify you individually.

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers provide options to block, delete, or receive alerts about cookies. To learn more about managing cookies, visit your browser's support page. By continuing to use our site without adjusting cookie settings, you accept our cookie usage as outlined in this policy.

For further information about our data collection and processing practices, please refer to our Privacy Policy. If you have any questions about our Cookie Policy, feel free to contact us via our website's contact page.
